Gizmo's Quest
Level Up Game Jam 2020

2D platformer game developed in Unity in the course of 32 hours with the theme "You are your own enemy".

You play the role of Gizmo, a mage trying to battle his own demons. The enemies you find along the way are small versions of you, past mistakes that you may choose to kill or make peace with. In the end, will you fuel your inner evil with hatred or will you make ammends?
